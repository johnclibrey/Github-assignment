My Favorite Recipe: Simple Brownies

These brownies are easy to make and super yummy!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Melt the butter in a bowl.
  3. Mix in the sugar, eggs, and vanilla.
  4. Add the cocoa powder, flour, and salt.
  5. Stir everything until smooth.
  6. Pour the batter into a greased pan.
  7. Bake for about 25–30 minutes.
